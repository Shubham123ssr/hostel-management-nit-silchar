Reason to Join NIT SILCHAR

FEST

Incandescence

The annual cultural fest of NIT Silchar. It is one of the most awaited events of the college. This falls in the month of February. Students from various college from all over the country participate in this festival.



Tecnoesis

Tecnoesis is the technical festival of NIT Silchar. This fest falls in the month of October. Here we see young and energetic minds showing their skills. Various events like Robotics, Paper Presentations, Quizzes & lots of exciting events take place for a span of three days.



Thundermarch

Thundermarch is the annual metal fest of NIT Silchar. It falls in the month of February-March along with Incandescence and features enthralling and earth-shaking performances by various metal bands from India as well as famous international bands. Previously headlined by Nale (TM'13) and Deathember (TM'14).



Posua

POSUA is the annual celebration of RONGALI BIHU in NIT Silchar that marks the beginning of the Hindu Solar Calender Year. It is a showcase of Assamese culture and features performances such as the traditional Bihu and other cultural performances by famous Assamese personalities. It is generally held in the month of April.

Asia's Biggest Digital LIBRARY

LIBRARY

The Central Library which is the heart of the Institute was established in 1977. It provides one of the important academic services to the Institute. It is a well-equipped Library, centrally located with easy access and does provide right impetus for the intellectual growth of the students, teachers, research scholars & others around. Presently, the Library is a part of the LTT building & having around 97,000 collections of documents of both print & non print materials. NIT Silchar has invested Rs. 44 Crore to create a world-class, innovative, inviting and flexible library system to enhance campus learning, support research, and to provide community outreach service having 90,000 sq. ft. carpet area. For this purpose, Central Library, NIT Silchar has entered into a collaborative MOU with University Library & Mortenson Centre for International Library Programme of University of Illinois at Urbana Chaimpaign, USA to create world class LIS facilities at NIT Silchar. The library building is in the final stage of completion.

PLACEMENT SCENARIO

PLACEMENT SCENARIO

Placement is one of the most important thing which comes in every students mind while filling choices for josaa counselling. The Placement scenario of NIT Silchar is experiencing an inclusive growth year-by-year. NIT Silchar is among one of few the colleges of India where regularly PSUs like PowerGrid, HPCL, Power System Operation Corporation(POSOCO), Bharat Electronics Limited (BEL), IOCL, Oil India etc visited for Campus recruitment. The continuous effort of hardworking students and faculties are the reason Recently NIT Silchar has been ranked among Top 20 Engineering colleges of India by highly reputed Times Higher Education(THE) WORLD RANKING and continuously touching the new heights in terms of Placements and Global Ranking .

SPORTS

Adventure Sports

The Institute has a playground measuring 150m X 90m with facility for playing football, Cricket, Hockey, and other games. The institute is also having a Sports Complex for providing various types of outdoor and indoor games to students of this Institute. NIT Silchar also has a hard court for playing basketball and is provided with hard courts for playing Volleyball and Badminton. It has got a Lawn Tennis court within the campus. Overall, the institute has sufficient facilities for games and sports. NIT Silchar has a dedicated number of people working hard to increase the overall enthusiasm for sports in the Institute and the smooth conduction of the tournament that occurs year round.
